Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 2026

Dungeon Calendar collects account, campaign, scheduling, and subscription information necessary to provide the service.

Information We Collect

We may collect your name, username, email address, optional phone number, profile settings, campaign membership, character names, availability responses, campaign settings, and subscription status.

How We Use Information

We use this information to operate Dungeon Calendar, manage campaigns, schedule sessions, save user settings, process subscription access, provide support, and improve reliability.

Firebase and Stripe

Dungeon Calendar uses Firebase for authentication and cloud storage. Stripe processes subscription payments. Dungeon Calendar does not store full credit card numbers.

Data Sharing

We do not sell personal information. Data may be shared with service providers necessary to operate the app, including Firebase and Stripe.

Account Updates and Deletion

You may update profile information in the app. For account deletion or privacy support, contact dungeoncalendarsupport@gmail.com.

Children's Privacy

Dungeon Calendar is not directed toward children under 13 without appropriate parental or guardian involvement.
